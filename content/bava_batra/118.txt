And if you think that only successful petitions were recorded, like the one of the daughters of Tzelophchad, then the petition of the descendants of Joseph will prove you wrong, for they <a href="http://bible.cc/joshua/17-15.htm">did not get it granted</a>.<br />
<br />
Joshua and Calev took the portions of the spies in the Land. We learn it from "<a href="http://bible.cc/numbers/14-38.htm">they lived from among the spies</a>." We already know that they did not die from "<a href="http://bible.cc/numbers/26-65.htm">and not a man was left of them except for Caleb and Joshua</a>," so what does the extra "live" tell us? That they lived in the portions of the spies.<br />
