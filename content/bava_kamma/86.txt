<div>
Can we derive the answer from this ruling: "One who strikes his father but doesn't inflict a wound is obligated in five payments?" - No! Here, he made his father bald with a depilatory cream, and his father used to dance in taverns.</div>
<div>
<br /></div>
<div>
One who humiliates a blind or a sleeping person is liable, but a sleeping person who humiliates someone is not liable. In sum, one is not liable for humiliation unless he <a href="http://bible.cc/deuteronomy/25-11.htm">intends to injure</a>.</div><div><br /></div><div>Art: Figures in the Tavern by Auguste de Wilde</div>
