The unlearned person's dough is presumed to be ritually impure, but the priest might think it pure since it comes from the sage. <br />
<br />
<div style="margin-bottom: 0in;">
In contrast, a Sage is allowed to knead the dough for the unlearned, then separate the priest's portion - but teach the unlearned not to touch the dough.</div>
