<br />
If five men wrote a common text in the Get, namely, "I, so-and-so, am divorcing my wife, so-and-so, etc.," the Get is valid for all five couples, and it should be given to every one of the women named in it to effect her divorce.<div><br /></div><div>Art: Two sisters by Theodore Chasseriau</div>
