<br />
Suppose one performs an act of betrothal and tells a woman, "Become betrothed to me after I convert to Judaism," or "after your husband dies," or "After your sister (my current wife) dies." In all these cases, she is not betrothed even after the specified event takes place.<div><br /></div><div>Art: A Beggar Woman by Hugues Merle</div>
