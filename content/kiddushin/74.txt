<br />A midwife is believed to tell us who is the firstborn – but only immediately after birth; his mother is believed for the first seven days of his life; his father is always believed because of “<a href="http://bible.cc/deuteronomy/21-17.htm">...shall recognize the firstborn</a>.”<br />
<br />
A mother of a child born out of wedlock is believed if she says that she cohabited with a person who is genealogically fit.<div><br /></div><div>Art: A Woman with her Baby in her Lap by Pieter de Hooch</div>
