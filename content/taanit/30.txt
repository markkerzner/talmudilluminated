Five good things happened on that day.<p></p><p>The Jews who were <a href="http://mkerzner.blogspot.com/2021/12/taanit-29.html" target="_blank">dying in the desert for forty years</a> stopped dying on this day.</p><p>The prohibition to give wives to the Binyamin tribe - which resulted from<a href="https://www.mechon-mamre.org/p/pt/pt0721.htm" target="_blank">&nbsp;the incident at Givah</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;was annulled on that day, and Jews were reunited.</p><p>The prohibition to go to Jerusalem, established by Yaravam ben Nevat, was removed.</p><p>The slain people of Bethar, who <a href="http://mkerzner.blogspot.com/2021/12/taanit-29.html" target="_blank">were killed on Tisha B'Av</a>, were allowed to be buried.
