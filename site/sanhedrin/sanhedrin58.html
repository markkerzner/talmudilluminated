Sanhedrin 58 - Noahide Marital Laws

The Noahide marital laws are derived from the verse, "Therefore shall a man leave his father and his mother and cling to his wife," but not to the wife of his fellow man, not to an animal, etc.

A Noahite is permitted to marry his daughter. You may ask why Adam did not marry his daughter? He did it so that Cain could marry his sister, based on "The world will be built through kindness" in Psalms. A Noahite is also permitted to marry his sister. Didn't we just say that "The world will be built through kindness" allowed Cain to marry his sister, but otherwise, it would be prohibited?! True, but once it was permitted to Cain, it remained permitted.
